Chicken Flautas

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas
  • 100 gr. of shredded chicken breast
  • Sour cream
  • 50 gr. of Cotija Cheese
  • Shredded Lettuce

Steps

  1. Add oil into a pan until the oil covers the surface
  2. Using the lowest flame possible, heat up the pan
  3. Wrap up some of the shredded chicken with the tortilla,it should look like a little rod
  4. Repeat that step until you have the needed amount of Tacos
  5. Put the tacos in the pan and let it sit in there until it gets crispy on that side, if its already crispy turn it to other side of the taco
  6. Keep turning the tacos until all of the sides of it are crispy
  7. Remove them from the pan and put them in a plate (Don't forget to turn off the stove)
  8. Add some sour cream on top of the tacos
  9. Sprinkle the Cotija Cheese on top of it
  10. To finish off the plate, put lettuce on top of everything
  11. There it is, now you have some delicious tacos dorados ready to eat
