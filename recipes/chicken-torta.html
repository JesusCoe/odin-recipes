Grilled Chicken Torta

Ingredients

  • Any bread you like is enough but, if you can get a telera bread, get it
  • 100 gr. of chicken breast
  • Mayonnaise
  • 4 Jalapeno peppers
  • A pinch of salt
  • A lemon
  • 1 Avocado

Steps

  1. Add a little bit of oil into a pan and then put it on the stove
  2. While the pan is heating up, season the chicken breast with salt
  3. Once the pan is heated up put the chicken in the pan and cook it until it is well done
  4. Remove the chicken out of the pan
  5. Slice the Jalapeno peppers into 8 pieces, throw it in the pan and keep stirring the peppers for about 4-5 minutes
  6. Turn off the stove
  7. Put the peppers in a bowl and squeeze the lemon juice
  8. Start to open the bread horizontally
  9. Cover the inside of the bread with Mayonnaise
  10. Put the chicken and the peppers inside of the bread
  11. Cut the avocado in half and get some slices of avocado
  12. Add the avocado into the Torta
  13. There it is! Your torta is done and ready to eat!
