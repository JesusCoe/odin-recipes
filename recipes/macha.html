Salsa Macha

Ingredients

  • 1 Liter of edible rapeseed oil
  • 30 Arbol peppers
  • 18 Peeled garlics
  • 4 Guajillo peppers
  • 50 Grams of natural natural peeled peanuts
  • A pinch of salt
  • 10 Grams of sesame seeds

Steps

  1. First of all, crush all of the garlics using a knife or your hands
  2. Wipe the Guajillo peppers clean. Take out all of the seeds and veins
  3. Add some of the rapeseed oil into a pan and use the lowest flame possible of your stove
  4. Let the pan to heat up for about 3 minutes and then throw all of the garlics into the pan
  5. Keep stirring the garlics until they reach a goldenish-brown color
  6. Once they reach that color, remove them from the pan
  7. Put all of the peppers in the pan and constantly stir them until the Arbol peppers reach a dark red color
  8. Once the peppers reached that color remove them from the pan
  9. Roast the peanuts for about 4 minutes
  10. Put everything in the blender, including the sesame seeds and the pinch of salt
  11. Once you blended it, store it in a jar
  12. Add rapeseed oil until it gets the consistency that you want
